About NovaColumn

NovaColumn collects Minecraft servers from the internet and logs data about their historical values.

What we collect:

  • Player count
  • Usernames & UUIDs
  • Server address and ports
  • Minecraft version
  • Signed messages status
  • Server icon

NovaColumn's data helps Minecraft server administrators optimize their servers and improve their player experience.

Have questions? Contact us at novacolumn@hotmail.com.
