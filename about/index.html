About NovaColumn

NovaColumn is an initiative dedicated to analyzing the Minecraft community. Our platform collects and logs historical data from publicly available Minecraft servers on the internet.

What data we collect:

  • Player count
  • Usernames & UUIDs
  • Server address and ports
  • Minecraft version
  • Signed message status
  • Server icon

To ensure the accuracy of our dataset, we require server owners to verify their ownership before requesting removal of their server from our database.

If you wish to request verification and deletion of data, please contact us at novacolumn@hotmail.com.
